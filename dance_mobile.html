Rhythm Ballet

Our Curriculum

Our studio provides a complete balltet education, with classes ranging from foundational technique to advanced repertoire and pointe work. We focus on:

  • Classical ballet technique at all levels
  • Pointe and variations training
  • Conditioning and supplemental classes
Our Instructors

Our team is comprised of experienced professionals with a deep commitment to classical ballet education. Each instructor brings a wealth of performance and teaching experience, guiding students with precision, passion, and personalized attention. We are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of ballet artists
